Fashion Nova Mobile Flow Redesign
January - March 2022

Overview

I analyzed the mobile design for the Fashion Nova, specifically the user flow for finding a pair of jeans. I rebranded and redesigned the flow, making a more streamlined and visually appealing process.

The Challenge

Fashion Nova's flow was clutterrd and lacked organization. To improve it, I needed to create clear filters and add intuitive ways for people to see and find what style of jeans they are looking for.

User Persona

The average Fashion Nova user is typically a female between the ages of 14 and 35. Fashion Nova caters to women who want to feel sexy, selling tight, stretchy clothing and clubwear. The user of this site is tech-savvy, they are active on multiple social media platforms and are very conscious about their online image and presence. Fashion Nova does most of its marketing through collaborations with celebrities and influencers such as Cardi B and Megan Thee Stallion. Fashion Nova shoppers actively keep up with the trends and want to look/dress like their favorite celebrities.

Pain Points

  • Confusing Web Layout
  • Lack of navigation for item specificity
  • Necessary information is not prominent
